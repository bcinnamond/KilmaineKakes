Welcome to Kilmaine Kakes

About Us

Kilmaine Kakes is a family-owned bakery dedicated to crafting exquisite cakes and pastries for all occasions. For over 30 years, our skilled bakers have poured their passion into creating delectable confections using time-honored recipes and the finest all-natural ingredients. From light and airy genoise to rich, fudgy layers, every bite is a flavor symphony that dances across the palate.

While taste reigns supreme, Kilmaine Kakes is also renowned for our breathtaking artistry. Our talented cake decorators are true masters, transforming each creation into an edible masterpiece. Whether an elegant wedding cake adorned with handcrafted sugar flowers or a whimsical children's cake brimming with imaginative details, our decorators bring visions to life. With an array of indulgent pastries like buttery croissants and silky crème brûlées, Kilmaine Kakes is the perfect destination to make any occasion extraordinary.
