How to Upload Images

Please access the website at https://imgbb.com/login and log in using the provided credentials:

  • Username: kilmainekakes Password: K1lma1n3KaK3s!

Once logged in, locate the upload button situated in the top right corner of the interface. Proceed to upload your desired image by dragging it into the designated area and providing a name and description. Please be mindful that the description will serve as alt text for the image.

After uploading, close any pop-up prompts and navigate to the gallery window. Click on the name of the uploaded image and scroll down to find its Image URL. Copy the URL.

Subsequently, paste the copied Image URL into the "Upload an Image" form on the website. It's important to ensure all images are cropped square to maintain correct formatting.
